QUESTIONS

How do I subscribe to Pro?
Inside the app. Download VerseFlow (free), create an account, then open Settings → Account → Upgrade to Pro. You'll pay via Paystack (Africa) or Lemon Squeezy (international) right there, and Pro unlocks on that device the moment your payment clears. Subscribing in the app keeps your payment tied to your account automatically.
Is the free version really free?
Yes — offline mode is free forever, no account needed. It's the full app minus the cloud/AI features that cost us to run.
Why is the price different by region?
We price fairly for local economies. African churches pay a discounted rate in local currency via Paystack; international churches pay via Lemon Squeezy in their own currency.
How many devices can use one Pro account?
One active device at a time. Signing in on a new device signs the previous one out — so your subscription stays yours.
Can I cancel anytime?
Anytime. You keep Pro until the end of the period you paid for, then drop back to the free offline tier — your songs and settings stay.
